### 1-2.数値を入力で奇数・偶数判定
＊＊スクリプト内容＊＊

read num

echo "Enter number: $num"

if \[ $((num % 2)) -eq 0 ]; then

echo "It's an even number"

else

echo "It's an odd number"

fi


実行結果：

Enter number: 2
It's an even number
